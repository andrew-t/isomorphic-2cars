Isomorphic 2Cars

Use the left arrow key (or tap the left side of the screen) to move the left car and move the spaceship horizontally. Use the right arrow key (or tap the right side of the screen) to move the right car and move the spaceship vertically.

Don't worry, mathematically you're only playing one game at a time.

Press space (or tap anywhere) to begin.
